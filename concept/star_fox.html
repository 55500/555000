Star Fox



Levels

Levels consist of multiple All Range Mode areas interconnected by bi-directional On-Rails sections. Levels designs will feature branching paths, circular paths, and linear paths. Players can explore levels openly. All Range Mode areas will have exits to On-Rails Sections leading to other All Range Mode areas.



Objectives

Multiple objectives will be present in each level. Some examples include destroying multiple generators, discovering a secret area leading to a boss battle, or flying through challenging terrain to trigger a dogfight.



Narrative Progression

Progression through levels is variable depending which objective is completed in each level. This will alter the narrative, allowing each playthrough to have some variety.
