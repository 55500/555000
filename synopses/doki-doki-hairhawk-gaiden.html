Doki-Doki Hairhawk Gaiden: School Love Story!

Your friendly neighborhood troublemaker Hairhawk has transferred to a new high school... AGAIN!

There's a new face at Omoi Shinzou Academy - it's Hairhawk! After accidentally joining the Heart Breaker's Club, our lovable badass discovers his more sensitive side. A new leaf turns over in Hairhawk's life, and this time he's not out for blood - he's out for love!?
